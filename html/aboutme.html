About me

I started taking pictures as a kid with Kodak disposable cameras. The stuff I would usually photograph back then, were my dog and cats in the garden.
Later on in high school I worked as an assistant photographer at a few weddings. However I truly discovered my passion when I got my first camera and started experimenting with all types of photography themes. I mostly like capturing interesting angles of buildings as well as interesting street photography moments. I do both color and B/W, but tend to always like more the B/W versions of my pictures.
Even though I do not have my own studio yet, I cooperate and even take on an event or a personal photoshoot.
